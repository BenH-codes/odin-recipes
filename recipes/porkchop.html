World's Best Honey Garlic Pork Chops

Juicy pockchops with a sweet garlic glaze, delicious!

Honey garlic pork chops are a perfect balance of sweet and savory. The honey provides a natural sweetness that is complemented bby the savory flavors of garlic and soy sauce. The pork chops themselves are juicy and tender, and absorb the flavors of the glaze beautifully. The cobination of these flavors creates a dish that is both satisfying and coforting, with a perfect blend of sweet and savory notes that will leave you wanting more. This is a quick and easy meal perfect for any night of the week.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup ketchup
  • 2 ⅔ tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons low-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 6 (4 ounce) (1-inch thick) pork chops

Steps

  1. Preheat grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate. Gather ingredients.
  2. Whisk ketchup, honey, soy sauce, and garlic together in a bowl to make a glaze.
  3. Sear the pork chops on both sides on the preheated grill. Lightly brush glaze onto each side of the chops as they cook; grill until no longer pink in the center, about 7 to 9 minutes per side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read 145 degrees F (63 degrees C).
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
